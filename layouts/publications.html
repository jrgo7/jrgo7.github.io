Apparent Age Estimation: Challenges and Outcomes

This is a data science project on apparent age estimation. Here, we looked into the IMDB-WIKI dataset (the largest dataset for apparent age estimation) and the Deep EXpectation (DEX) method used to finetune a VGG-16 model for the age estimation task.

After replicating the original model, we then tried to improve on it by exploring further finetuning on combinations of other known apparent age estimation datasets, such as ChaLearn LAP, APPA-REAL, and FairFace; we also explored replacing the cross-entropy loss function with mean-variance loss and adaptive mean-residue loss.

So Near, Yet So Far: How Violence Against Women Blocks the Philippines from True Gender Equality

Before I became an undergraduate computer science student in DLSU, I was a student in Saint Jude Cathoic School (SJCS). While there, I was able to win the Harvard Alumni for Global Women's Empowerment essay writing contest in 2022.

My piece essentially expressed concern on the ironic situation of the Philippines in terms of women empowerment: despite consistently ranking as the best country in Asia in terms of closing the gender gap, violance against women remains prevalent in our nation. I proposed that just as the colonial powers have forced their way into the country, so too did their societal norms, which have overwritten our feminist precolonial past.
