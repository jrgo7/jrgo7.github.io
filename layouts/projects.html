An Analysis on Competitive Pokémon Teams

This was a complete data science project applied to Pokémon Showdown which involved data wrangling, exploratory data analysis, data mining, and statistical inferences, made to determine patterns in team building, metagame trends, and strategic preferences that can be observed from Pokemon Showdown's Gen 5 OU match data.

We found that teams tend to be built around the concept of controlling the weather, no matter the skill level (measured via ELO) of the players. Despite being based on an older generation of Pokémon games, the strategies of each player (the metagame) still evolves over time. This project was made for CSMODEL, a data science course in DLSU.
